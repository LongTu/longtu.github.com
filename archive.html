SSL/TLS

During the summer of 2013, I built a Certificate Discovery Tool that is able to connect SSL/TLS/starttls (XMPP&Ldap) servers and obtain X.509 Certificate information.
This software hacked the SSL/TLS three-way handshake in the middle by sending fake handshake requests. It helped automatize the verification process of back-end teams and eliminated the unexpected certificate expiration issue for all internal servers.
